Meet John

Driven By Craftsmanship & Care

Beginning his journey in construction at age 15, John Rose quickly distinguished himself through dedication and natural talent, advancing to lead hand for a prominent general contracting company. With 25 years of carpentry experience, 15 years in professional painting, and specialized expertise in flooring installation, John's comprehensive background ensures superior craftsmanship in every project.

In 2019, driven by a vision for higher quality standards in the industry, John established JR's Painting and Home Renovation. What began as a part-time venture grew into a thriving full-time business by 2023, built on a foundation of exceptional workmanship and unwavering attention to detail.

Today, John's passion for transforming spaces through expert painting and renovation continues to drive the company's success, creating lasting relationships within the Pembroke community through reliable service and personalized care.
